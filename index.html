The bottom line is everyone needs underwear

Smalls For All is a UK based charity which helps women and children in rural Africa, meet their most basic hygiene and security needs.

After paying for essential living costs, many can't afford to buy underwear and can fall victim to disease or violent attacks. And girls who are lucky enough to have a place in school, often miss 3 or 4 days a month due their lack of smalls.

It's hard to believe that something as simple as providing underwear can help. It really does make a big difference. Smalls For All donates underwear in many ways - through links with established charities; through hospitals, IDP campss, children's homes and orphanages.
